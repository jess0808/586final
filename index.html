Who is Doug?

Doug is a 2 year old pug living in Nashville with his owner Leslie Mosier. Leslie quit her job to become her pug's manager when she saw how much attention Doug was getting on social media after she posted pictures and videos of him with silly props. Doug loves posing for pictures and has taken the internet by storm. He has even reached "celebrity status" as he's considered one of the most famous animals in the world and has made appearances in music videos and met many famous celebrities.
